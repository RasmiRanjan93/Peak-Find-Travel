Peak & Find

Peak and Find welcomes you to the lap of nature with our seamless and integrated Camping and Villas organized by the young enthusiast. Situated in the middle of lush greenery with a serene view, it is one of those places that immediately gets you rid of the chaos that big cities might have carried over to your vacation. Peak and Find provides you with the best recreational outdoor activities like watching sundown behind the mountains and star gazing in the open air.

Peak & Find

Coming up with the new wave in experiencing nature, doing something sporty or just relaxing with great food and drink we aim at creating an idea to live free. Embracing everyone from six-year-olds, 60-year-olds, solo trips, couples, groups, corporates and families we bring a joyous platform to fulfill your stay. We are one-of-a-kind to redefine creating a memorable experience rather than calling it just a trip

Why choose Us

We at Peak and Find help our visitors explore the picturesque sites in and around Pawna Lake and Lonavala. The area is covered with lush greenery with a crystal-like lake. All energetic and enthusiastic minds come here to explore the mesmerizing scenes and trekking and we just guide them and provide the much-needed appliances to them. So with us, all you need to do is enjoy and the rest of the things will be taken care of by our crew members. We are here to your journey hassle-free and sublime. Tourists and visitors get to pick packages to power their whims and requirements.
